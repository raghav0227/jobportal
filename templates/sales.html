TOP SALES COMPANIES

Nalanda Learning System

Nalanda Learning Systems Pvt Ltd is a pre-primary education initiative, founded in 2012, under the brand name ‘Little Laureates’. Nalanda Learning’s mission is to establish the best preschool ecosystem in India, with special emphasis on curriculum, content and technology.

Mahindra Finance

Mahindra & Mahindra Financial Services Limited (Mahindra Finance) has come a long way since its humble beginnings in 1991. From our very first steps as a captive finance company for Mahindra & Mahindra, we have grown and evolved to meet the diverse needs of our cherished customers. Today, we stand tall as a leading non-banking financial company (NBFC) in India, with over 9 million happy customers by our side.

DCB BANK

DCB Bank is a new generation private sector bank with 440 branches (as on 31st December 2023) across India. It is a scheduled commercial bank regulated by the Reserve Bank of India. It is professionally managed and governed. DCB Bank has contemporary technology and infrastructure including state of the art internet banking for personal as well as business banking customers.












GITLAB

GitLab is an AI-powered code collaboration platform. They've been remote since day one. It was eshtabilished to continue the development of the open-source code-sharing platform launched in 2011 by Dmytro Zaporozhets.










ADOBE

Adobe is a software company that offers different tools and software to create, edit, share, scan and sign digital documents. Their popular products are Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Lightroom and Adobe Premiere Rush. Many candidates prefer working at Adobe due to work-life balance, job security and company culture. Adobe often hires professionals for different work-from-home roles, such as senior product designer, senior solution consultant, program manager, Adobe analyst architect and Adobe experience manager.

DELL TECH.

Dell Technologies is a multinational company that assists customers in building their digital infrastructure. They also sell computers and other electronic equipment. Dell Technologies provides companies with cybersecurity and data protection services. Candidates who want to join Dell Technologies can apply for different remote job roles, such as senior service delivery engineer, transport network services consultant, edge product manager, software principal engineer and senior technician.
