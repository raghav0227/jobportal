Remote companies are businesses that have a workforce that primarily works from outside of a traditional office setting. This means employees can work from home, co-working spaces, or any location with a good internet connection. There are a few different terms used to describe this type of work arrangement, including:

  • Remote work
  • Telecommuting
  • Virtual job
  • Home-based job
These terms all basically mean the same thing: a job that doesn't require you to be in a physical office every day. Some companies are flily remote, meaning all of their employees work remotely. Others are hybrid, with some employees working remotely and others working in an office.
