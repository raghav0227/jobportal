Marketing jobs are all about promoting a company's products or services to generate interest and sales. It's a broad field, so there are many different specializations, but here's a breakdown of two main categories:

    Traditional Marketing: This is the kind of marketing you might think of first, like print ads, TV commercials, and brand building. Some common traditional marketing jobs include:
  • Marketing Manager: Oversees all marketing activities for a company or product.
  • Brand Manager: Develops and maintains a specific brand image.
  • Advertising Account Executive: Manages client accounts and creates advertising campaigns.
  • Market Researcher: Studies target markets to inform marketing strategies.


    Digital Marketing: This is all about using digital channels to reach consumers, like social media, email marketing, and search engines. Here are some popular digital marketing jobs:
  • Social Media Manager: Creates and manages a company's social media presence.
  • Content Marketing Manager: Develops and executes content strategies to attract and engage customers.
  • SEO Specialist: Optimizes websites to rank higher in search engine results.
  • Email Marketing Specialist: Creates and sends email campaigns to promote products or services.
