MNC jobs refer to positions in Multinational Corporations (MNCs). These are companies with operations and offices in multiple countries around the world. Here's what MNC jobs can offer:

  • Diverse work environment: You'll collaborate with colleagues from various backgrounds, fostering intercultural communication and teamwork skills.
  • Career growth opportunities: MNCs often invest heavily in employee training and development, providing opportunities to advance your career.
  • Global exposure: Depending on the role, you might have the chance to work on international projects or even get relocated to another country.
To land an MNC job, you'll likely need strong skills in communication, teamwork, and problem-solving. Being proficient in a foreign language can also be a big advantage.


TOP MNC COMPANIES

Samsung Group

A South Korean multinational conglomerate company headquartered in Samsung Town, Seoul. It comprises numerous subsidiaries and affiliated businesses across various industries including electronics, home appliances, semiconductors, chipsets, aerospace, construction, finance, healthcare, IT services, and entertainment.


Amazon

One of the biggest names in the world, Amazon is a favorite and popular recruiter in the country. With almost 800,000 employees, there are various roles to choose from the software developers, testers, virtual assistants, customer support, logistics, and sales. For the Indian subcontinent, Amazon provides a separate Virtual Customer Service (VCS) making anyone with basic communication skills apply and work remotely from India.



Microsoft Corporation

An American multinational technology corporation that develops, manufactures, licenses, supports, and sells computer software, consumer electronics, personal computers, and related services.








Apple Inc.

This American multinational technology company designs, develops, and sells consumer electronics, computer software, and online services.










Alphabet Inc. (Google)

An American multinational technology conglomerate that specializes in Internet-related services and products, which include online advertising technologies, a search engine, cloud computing, software, and hardware.








DELL TECH.

Dell Technologies is a multinational company that assists customers in building their digital infrastructure. They also sell computers and other electronic equipment. Dell Technologies provides companies with cybersecurity and data protection services. Candidates who want to join Dell Technologies can apply for different remote job roles, such as senior service delivery engineer, transport network services consultant, edge product manager, software principal engineer and senior technician.
